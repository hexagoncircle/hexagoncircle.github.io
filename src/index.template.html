About me

I'm a passionate front-end developer that has always worked to blur the line between design and development, driving user experience with positive interactivity and thoughtful interfaces. I believe the web should be transparent while providing perpetual consumer delight. Applications and tools are to be accessible to all, anywhere, at any time. I value constructive feedback and working with diverse, inclusive teams that share a similar foundation.
